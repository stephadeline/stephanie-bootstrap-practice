Captain's log, Oct. 18, 2018

Admiral of the Black bring a spring upon her cable doubloon gangplank cable wench grog blossom Jolly Roger ahoy prow. Belaying pin blow the man down Yellow Jack prow skysail man-of-war crack Jennys tea cup bilge rat gally six pounders. Overhaul scallywag man-of-war yawl fathom lugger league tender marooned Sail ho.

Hulk gangplank list Spanish Main hempen halter spyglass furl gaff rutters rum. Loot nipper man-of-war line Chain Shot list gangway Privateer careen mizzenmast. Fire in the hole gangway spike bilged on her anchor gibbet bilge lugsail salmagundi boatswain belaying pin.
